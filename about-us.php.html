About Us

JRK Engineers is our commitment to bring professionalism, good service and trust to the home repair service and maintenance business. We take immense pride in sending some of the most professional handymen to your homes to fix things that aren't working.
We have fixed over 5,50,000 houses in our little over 5 years of existence so far.
